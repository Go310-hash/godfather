About Our School

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops well-rounded individuals ready for higher education and the world of work.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in academic excellence, discipline, and character formation in the North West Region and across Cameroon.

Why Choose PCHS?

Highly qualified staff, modern facilities, outstanding exam results, and a safe learning environment. Day and boarding from Form One to Upper Sixth.

Our Story

Progressive Comprehensive High School was founded in 1968 in Bamenda, North West Region of Cameroon.

Under the vision of our late founder Bobe Yong Francis, and as part of Yong’s Educational Establishments, PCHS has grown into an institution of great repute in Cameroon, recording outstanding results in public examinations year after year.

Our commitment remains to make quality education accessible for all—combining academic excellence with discipline, character formation, and hands-on skills. Today we serve both day and boarding students from Form One through Upper Sixth, and have expanded to include a Technical-Industrial Section at our extended campus at Veterinary Junction, Bamenda.

Programs

We offer full secondary education and a growing Technical-Industrial stream to prepare students for both university and the world of work.

General Secondary

Form One to Upper Sixth (day and boarding), with a strong focus on G.C.E. Ordinary and Advanced Level preparation and all core subjects.

Technical-Industrial Section

At our Veterinary Junction campus: Building Construction, Electricity, Woodwork, Motor Mechanics, and Home Economics. Same high standards, practical skills for the future.
